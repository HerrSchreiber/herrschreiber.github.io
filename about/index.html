All about me!

My name is Rob Schreiber and I've been teaching at TVMSC for 4 (and counting) years. I initially taught AP Physics B, AP Computer Science, & Conceptual Physics, but since TVMSC has grown so much, I've been allowed to offer some extended classes in physics & computer science: AP Physics C & Advanced Computer Science.

Before working at TVMSC, I got my bachelor's of science in both Mathematics & Physics at Boise State University and worked as a research assistant there. I mainly worked on automating physics experiments and assisting other researchers with their instrumentation.

When I'm not teaching, I like to watch Netflix, play video games, and work on my thousand or so half-finished programming projects (or more likely, start my 1001st one.).
